**At a glance:** 30+ servers across 6 subcategories. No official Epic/Cerner servers yet. **Rating: 4/5.**

## FHIR / EHR Integration

- **[wso2/fhir-mcp-server](https://github.com/wso2/fhir-mcp-server)** (98 stars, Python, Apache-2.0) — Expose any FHIR server as MCP. Full CRUD, SMART-on-FHIR auth, stdio/SSE/HTTP.
- **[health-record-mcp](https://github.com/jmandel/health-record-mcp)** (75 stars, TypeScript) — Innovative: grep, SQL, and JavaScript query tools over EHR data.
- **[the-momentum/fhir-mcp-server](https://github.com/the-momentum/fhir-mcp-server)** (68 stars, Python) — 11 tools with Pinecone vector search for clinical documents.
- **AWS HealthLake MCP** (part of awslabs/mcp 8.5k stars) — 11 tools, automatic datastore discovery, read-only mode, 96% test coverage.
- **[langcare-mcp-fhir](https://github.com/langcare/langcare-mcp-fhir)** (15 stars, Go) — Enterprise EHR proxy for Epic/Cerner/GCP. TLS 1.3, PHI scrubbing, 40+ clinical skills library.
- **[medplum-mcp](https://github.com/rkirkendall/medplum-mcp)** (14 stars, TypeScript) — 33 tools, most tool-rich FHIR server.

## Medical Research

- **[PubMed-MCP-Server](https://github.com/JackKuo666/PubMed-MCP-Server)** (104 stars, Python) — Most starred research MCP. Search, metadata, PDF download, deep paper analysis.
- **[clinicaltrialsgov-mcp-server](https://github.com/cyanheads/clinicaltrialsgov-mcp-server)** (58 stars, TypeScript) — 7 tools: trend analysis, patient-to-trial matching, study comparison. 92% test coverage.
- **[medical-mcps](https://github.com/pascalwhoop/medical-mcps)** (14 stars, Python) — **100+ tools across 14 biomedical APIs**: PubMed, OpenFDA, KEGG, UniProt, GWAS, ChEMBL, and more.

## Healthcare Hubs

- **[healthcare-mcp-public](https://github.com/Cicatriiz/healthcare-mcp-public)** (101 stars, JavaScript) — 9 tools: FDA drugs, PubMed, clinical trials, ICD-10, DICOM, medical calculator.
- **[medical-mcp](https://github.com/JamesANZ/medical-mcp)** (75 stars, TypeScript) — 15 tools across FDA/WHO/PubMed/RxNorm. **Zero API keys needed.**

## Medical Imaging

**[dicom-mcp](https://github.com/ChristianHinge/dicom-mcp)** (86 stars, Python) — 11 tools for DICOM/PACS: query patients/studies/series, move data between nodes, extract PDF reports.

## Drug & Pharmacology

- **[drugbank-mcp-server](https://github.com/openpharma-org/drugbank-mcp-server)** — 17,430+ drugs, interactions, pathways, structure search via SQLite
- **[nlm-codes-mcp](https://github.com/openpharma-org/nlm-codes-mcp)** — ICD-10/ICD-11/HCPCS/LOINC/RxTerms via NLM Clinical Tables API

## What's Missing

- No official EHR vendor servers (Epic, Cerner)
- Imaging limited to DICOM metadata, no image analysis
- Genomics nearly absent
- HIPAA compliance documentation spotty across most projects

## The Bottom Line

Every major healthcare data domain covered. FHIR has genuine competition (6+ implementations). Research servers are production-ready. Vendor participation strong (AWS, WSO2, Innovaccer, LangCare). Held back by no official EHR vendor servers, imaging stops at metadata, and compliance documentation gaps.

**Rating: 4/5**
