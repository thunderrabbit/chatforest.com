**At a glance:** 50+ servers across HRIS, ATS, payroll, and recruiting. Surprisingly broad, but low adoption (highest: 14 stars). **Rating: 3.5/5.**

## HRIS Platforms

- **[acalder-techpm/bamboohr-mcp](https://github.com/acalder-techpm/bamboohr-mcp)** (3 stars, TypeScript, MIT, 74 tools) — Most comprehensive BambooHR server. 11 modules, 30 role-based workflow templates. BambooHR has **8 total implementations** — the most of any HR platform.
- **[aiadiguru2025/sf-mcp](https://github.com/aiadiguru2025/sf-mcp)** (1 star, Python, MIT, 43 tools) — SAP SuccessFactors with 13 categories, 21 data centers, enterprise security (connection pooling, OData injection prevention, credential masking).
- **[CDataSoftware/workday-mcp-server-by-cdata](https://github.com/CDataSoftware/workday-mcp-server-by-cdata)** (8 stars, Java) — Read-only JDBC gateway. CData also covers ADP, Greenhouse, Bullhorn, Paylocity.
- **[passgage/mcp-server](https://github.com/passgage/mcp-server)** (1 star, TypeScript, MIT) — **Official** vendor server, **130+ tools** across 25 services, dual auth, permission-aware.

## Applicant Tracking

- **[vanman2024/cats-mcp-server](https://github.com/vanman2024/cats-mcp-server)** (1 star, Python, **228 tools**) — Most tool-dense ATS server. Complete CATS API v3 coverage across 17 toolsets.
- **[dewierwan/ashby-mcp](https://github.com/dewierwan/ashby-mcp)** (TypeScript, 20+ tools) — Resume extraction, pipeline visibility, bulk operations, interview scheduling. 3 competing Ashby implementations exist.

## Payroll

- **[check-technologies/mcp-server-check](https://github.com/check-technologies/mcp-server-check)** (14 stars, Python, **263 tools**) — **Highest-starred HR MCP.** Official Check server, 17 toolsets, sandbox environment, read-only mode. Early Access Beta.
- **[JonasDNielsen/deel-mcp-server](https://github.com/JonasDNielsen/deel-mcp-server)** (TypeScript, MIT, 25 tools) — Global payroll via Deel API, read-only.

## Recruiting Intelligence

- **[dominicholcomb/mcp_candidate_evaluation](https://github.com/dominicholcomb/mcp_candidate_evaluation)** (1 star, Python) — Two-LLM bias-reduction architecture for candidate evaluation. Benchmarked across 6,480+ trials.
- **[WouterArtsRecruitin/recruitin-mcp-servers](https://github.com/WouterArtsRecruitin/recruitin-mcp-servers)** — Commercial suite, 43+ servers covering CV parsing, matching, and CRM.

## What's Missing

No LinkedIn Recruiter, no Indeed, no Lever, no iCIMS, no employee engagement (Lattice, Culture Amp, 15Five), no performance management, no LMS, no benefits administration.

## The Bottom Line

Broader than expected but shallower than needed. Check Payroll (263 tools, official), BambooHR (8 implementations), SAP SuccessFactors (enterprise-grade), and CATS ATS (228 tools) show real depth. But the highest-starred server has just 14 stars, major platforms are absent, and the ecosystem skews toward data access over intelligent workflows.

**Rating: 3.5/5**
