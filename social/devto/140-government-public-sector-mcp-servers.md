**At a glance:** 40+ servers across 8 subcategories. 5 official government agency servers. **Rating: 4/5.**

Five government agencies have released official MCP servers — more institutional adoption than any other vertical category. The U.S. has the deepest coverage, from official Census Bureau and GPO servers to a community mega-aggregator with 188+ tools across 36 APIs.

## Official Agency Servers

- **GovInfo MCP (U.S. GPO)** — first official U.S. federal MCP server. Bills, laws, Federal Register, CFR, presidential documents. Certified digital repository.
- **U.S. Census Bureau** (34 stars) — 3 tools with PostgreSQL caching. ACS, Decennial Census, Economic Census.
- **data.gouv.fr (France)** (85 stars) — most-starred official government MCP. Public hosted instance at mcp.data.gouv.fr.
- **India NSO eSankhyiki** — 7 datasets from Ministry of Statistics via FastMCP 3.0.
- **GSA-TTS USASpending** — demo with login.gov OIDC authentication.

## Mega-Aggregators

**lzinga/us-gov-open-data-mcp** — 188+ tools spanning 36+ APIs: Treasury, FRED, Congress, FDA, CDC, FEC, OSHA, Housing, Patents, Lobbying, and more. 18 APIs need no key. The "if you want one government MCP server" pick.

## Legislative & Congressional

- **European Parliament MCP** (61 tools, Apache 2.0) — OSINT with MEP influence scoring, coalition analysis, voting anomaly detection. 1,130+ unit tests.
- **LegiScan MCP** — all 50 states + Congress. 90% API usage reduction via batching.
- **CongressMCP** — 92 operations, hosted at congressmcp.lawgiver.ai.

## Census & Demographics

- **Open Census MCP** — natural language queries with fitness-for-use constraints
- **Google Data Commons MCP** — 190+ countries, census/climate/economics/health

## Open Data Portals

- **Data.gov MCP** (34 stars) — CKAN API, 4 tools for federal datasets
- **Socrata** — official + community implementations for thousands of government portals

## Procurement

- **Capture MCP** — SAM.gov + USASpending.gov integration
- **GovTribe** — commercial GovCon intelligence, updates every 15 min
- International: servers for 25+ countries including India, Turkey, Ukraine

## Tax & Elections

- **IRS Taxpayer MCP** — 39 tools, federal/state calculations through TY2025, all local
- **FEC MCP** — campaign finance research: candidates, contributions, expenditures

## What's Missing

Municipal/city services, voting/elections admin, social services, immigration, public transit, emergency management, most G20 nations.

## The Bottom Line

**Rating: 4/5** — Institutional adoption by five government agencies is remarkable for a protocol this young. Legislative and procurement coverage is comprehensive. The 188-tool US government server alone justifies the category. International coverage beyond US/France/India is thin.
