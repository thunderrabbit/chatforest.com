**At a glance:** 40+ servers, 8 subcategories, 5 official agency servers. **Rating: 4/5.**

## Official Agency Servers

- **GovInfo (U.S. GPO)** — first official federal MCP server. Bills, laws, Federal Register, CFR.
- **U.S. Census Bureau** (34 stars) — ACS, Decennial, Economic Census with PostgreSQL caching.
- **data.gouv.fr (France)** (85 stars) — public hosted instance at mcp.data.gouv.fr.
- **India NSO eSankhyiki** — 7 datasets, FastMCP 3.0.
- **GSA-TTS USASpending** — login.gov OIDC auth demo.

## Mega-Aggregator

**us-gov-open-data-mcp** — 188+ tools, 36+ APIs (Treasury, FRED, Congress, FDA, CDC, FEC, OSHA). 18 APIs need no key.

## Legislative

- **European Parliament MCP** — 61 tools, OSINT with MEP influence scoring, 1,130+ tests
- **LegiScan** — all 50 states + Congress
- **CongressMCP** — 92 operations, hosted service

## Other Categories

- **Census:** Open Census (natural language queries), Google Data Commons (190+ countries)
- **Open Data:** Data.gov CKAN, Socrata portals
- **Procurement:** SAM.gov, GovTribe, servers for India/Turkey/Ukraine
- **Tax:** IRS Taxpayer MCP (39 tools, federal/state through TY2025)
- **Elections:** FEC campaign finance research

## The Bottom Line

**Rating: 4/5** — Five government agencies officially supporting MCP is remarkable. The 188-tool US government server and European Parliament OSINT are standouts. International coverage beyond US/France/India needs work.
