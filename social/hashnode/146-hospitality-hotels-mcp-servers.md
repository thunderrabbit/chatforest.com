**At a glance:** 25+ servers across 6 subcategories. Consumer booking well-covered, enterprise hospitality completely absent. **Rating: 3.5/5.**

## Vacation Rentals

- **[openbnb-org/mcp-server-airbnb](https://github.com/openbnb-org/mcp-server-airbnb)** (393 stars, TypeScript, MIT) — Most popular hospitality MCP. 2 tools, Airbnb search via HTML scraping, DXT format, no API key needed.

## Hotel Booking

- **[jinkoso/jinko-mcp](https://github.com/jinkoso/jinko-mcp)** (TypeScript) — 5 tools, **2M+ hotels worldwide** with actual booking. Commercial API key required.
- **[soren-olympus/amadeus-mcp](https://github.com/soren-olympus/amadeus-mcp)** / **[fiqcodes/amadeus-mcp-server](https://github.com/fiqcodes/amadeus-mcp-server)** — Amadeus Travel API for hotels, flights, tours.

## Restaurant Reservations

- **[jrklein343-svg/restaurant-mcp](https://github.com/jrklein343-svg/restaurant-mcp)** (TypeScript, 12+ tools) — Unified **Resy + OpenTable** search with direct booking and reservation sniping.
- **[musemen/resy-mcp-server](https://github.com/musemen/resy-mcp-server)** (Python) — Resy automation with encrypted tokens and waitlist management.

## Review & Discovery

- **[Yelp/yelp-mcp](https://github.com/Yelp/yelp-mcp)** — **Official** Yelp server. Fusion AI API, natural language search, conversational reservations.
- **[pab1it0/tripadvisor-mcp](https://github.com/pab1it0/tripadvisor-mcp)** (46 stars, Python, MIT) — TripAdvisor Content API.

## What's Missing

No property management systems, no revenue management, no guest experience platforms, no food delivery, no hotel loyalty programs, no housekeeping/operations.

## The Bottom Line

Consumer booking well-covered: Airbnb (393 stars), 2M+ hotel booking via Jinko, unified Resy + OpenTable, official Yelp. Enterprise hospitality completely absent. Serves travelers excellently but hoteliers not at all.

**Rating: 3.5/5**
