**At a glance:** 20+ servers. Unified farm data, agricultural weather, market data, smallholder AI, livestock, satellite observation.

## Farm Data Platforms

**[Leaf Agriculture MCP](https://withleaf.io/en/whats-new/leaf-mcp-launch/)** — only commercial vendor with official ag MCP server. Unified API across John Deere, Climate FieldView, CNHi, AGCO, Trimble.

**John Deere** — two community implementations: [john-deere-ops-mcp](https://glama.ai/mcp/servers/@CoreyFransen08/john-deere-ops-mcp) (Cloudflare Workers, double OAuth proxy) and [ag-mcp](https://github.com/easavin/ag-mcp) (chat interface).

## Agricultural Weather

**[agri-weather-mcp](https://github.com/etudelab/agri-weather-mcp)** — soil temp/moisture at multiple depths, evapotranspiration, crop-specific alerts. **[gap-agriculture-mcp](https://github.com/eagleisbatman/gap-agriculture-mcp)** — Kenya/East Africa satellite forecasts.

## Market Data & Smallholder AI

**[agrobr-mcp](https://github.com/bruno-portfolio/agrobr-mcp)** — 19 Brazilian agricultural data sources (CEPEA, B3, CONAB, IBGE, NASA POWER).

**[FarmerChat-MCP](https://github.com/digitalgreenorg/DG_Open)** — 12 interconnected servers. Soil + weather + groundwater + elevation + satellite data for farmers in India, Ethiopia, Kenya, Nigeria, Brazil.

## Livestock & Satellites

**[nsip-api-client](https://github.com/epicpast/nsip-api-client)** — 15 tools for sheep breeding genetics. **[Axion-MCP](https://github.com/Dhenenjay/Axion-MCP)** — Google Earth Engine, 30+ datasets, NDVI crop health.

## What's Good

Leaf's official unified farm API. FarmerChat's ambitious multi-source advisory. Farming-specific weather data generic servers lack.

## What's Not

No equipment manufacturer official servers. No crop disease/pest ID. No irrigation management. Only sheep for livestock.

## Rating: 3.0/5

Early-stage with genuine substance. Agriculture is a $3 trillion industry where better information directly translates to higher yields.
