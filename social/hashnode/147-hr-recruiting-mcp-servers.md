**At a glance:** 50+ servers across HRIS, ATS, payroll, and recruiting. Surprisingly broad, but low adoption (highest: 14 stars). **Rating: 3.5/5.**

## HRIS Platforms

- **[acalder-techpm/bamboohr-mcp](https://github.com/acalder-techpm/bamboohr-mcp)** (3 stars, TypeScript, MIT, 74 tools) — Most comprehensive BambooHR server. 11 modules, 30 role-based workflow templates. **8 total implementations.**
- **[aiadiguru2025/sf-mcp](https://github.com/aiadiguru2025/sf-mcp)** (Python, MIT, 43 tools) — SAP SuccessFactors with 13 categories, 21 data centers, enterprise security.
- **[passgage/mcp-server](https://github.com/passgage/mcp-server)** (TypeScript, MIT) — **Official** vendor server, **130+ tools** across 25 services.

## Applicant Tracking

- **[vanman2024/cats-mcp-server](https://github.com/vanman2024/cats-mcp-server)** (Python, **228 tools**) — Most tool-dense ATS server. Complete CATS API v3 coverage.
- **[dewierwan/ashby-mcp](https://github.com/dewierwan/ashby-mcp)** (TypeScript, 20+ tools) — Resume extraction, pipeline visibility, bulk operations.

## Payroll

- **[check-technologies/mcp-server-check](https://github.com/check-technologies/mcp-server-check)** (14 stars, Python, **263 tools**) — **Highest-starred HR MCP.** Official Check server, 17 toolsets, sandbox environment.
- **[JonasDNielsen/deel-mcp-server](https://github.com/JonasDNielsen/deel-mcp-server)** (TypeScript, MIT, 25 tools) — Global payroll via Deel, read-only.

## Recruiting Intelligence

- **[dominicholcomb/mcp_candidate_evaluation](https://github.com/dominicholcomb/mcp_candidate_evaluation)** — Two-LLM bias-reduction architecture, benchmarked across 6,480+ trials.

## What's Missing

No LinkedIn Recruiter, no Indeed, no Lever, no iCIMS, no employee engagement (Lattice, Culture Amp), no performance management, no LMS.

## The Bottom Line

Broader than expected but shallower than needed. Check Payroll (263 tools), BambooHR (8 implementations), SAP SuccessFactors (enterprise-grade), and CATS ATS (228 tools) show real depth. But major platforms are absent and the ecosystem skews toward data access over intelligent workflows.

**Rating: 3.5/5**
