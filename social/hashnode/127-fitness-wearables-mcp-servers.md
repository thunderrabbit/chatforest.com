**At a glance:** 40+ fitness and wearables MCP servers. **Rating: 4/5.**

**Headlines:** Open Wearables (915 stars) unifies 6 wearable ecosystems. Garmin has 96+ tools. Strava has 7+ servers. WHOOP has 6 across 3 languages.

## Strava (7+ servers)

- **eddmann/strava-mcp** — 24 tools covering activities, segments, routes, training, gear
- **r-huijts/strava-mcp** — zero-install via npx
- **kw510/strava-mcp** — Cloudflare Workers, fully cloud-hosted

## Garmin Connect

- **Taxuspt/garmin_mcp** — 96+ tools, ~89% of python-garminconnect. Deepest single-device MCP.
- **Nicolasvegam/garmin-connect-mcp** — 61 tools across 7 categories, more curated.

## WHOOP (6+ servers)

**nissand/whoop-mcp-server-claude** leads with 18+ endpoints and full OAuth. Implementations in TypeScript, Ruby, and Go.

## Open Wearables (915 stars)

Evolved from Apple Health bridge to unified platform: Apple Health, Garmin, Polar, Suunto, WHOOP, Samsung Health Connect. Companion app, DuckDB backend, self-hosted.

## Oura Ring

**hemantkamalakar/oura-mcp-server** — 20 tools with anomaly detection, trend prediction, correlation analysis.

## Training & Multi-Platform

- **ai-endurance/mcp** — ML race predictions for runners/cyclists/triathletes
- **Async-IO/pierre_mcp_server** — 150+ wearables via Terra API, MCP + A2A + REST

## What's Missing

No Peloton, Zwift, Apple Watch direct, standardized health data format, or real-time streaming.

## The Bottom Line

**Rating: 4/5** — Broad and growing fast. Open Wearables leads unification. Main weakness: fragmentation requiring multiple servers for multi-device users.
